Michelle's Favourite Restaurant in Toronto

Enoteca Sociale:

Enoteca Sociale is an authentic Italian restaurant that is located in downtown Toronto. As I'm a big fan of Italian food, I was very excited to come here for the very first time. I remember distinctly ordering arancini for my appetizer and gnocchi for my main. Both dishes were absolutely delicious and it was hands down the best Italian restaurant I had ever been to. To finish up my meal, I ordered some cannoli for dessert and they were very light and creamy. I was definitely impressed with how all the food turned out!
